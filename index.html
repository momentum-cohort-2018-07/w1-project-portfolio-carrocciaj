All About Josh

Josh Carroccia is from a small town in South Central Montana called Melville. He grew up on a ranch nestled deep into the Crazy Mountains where he enjoyed camping, hiking, fishing and riding horses.

He moved out to Durham because of the tech that is in the surrounding area. He has been working in the tech industry for 3 years and wanted to extend his technical knowledge by becoming a software developer.

He also enjoys binge watching tv shows, playing first person shooters and RPGs and playing with his dog, Zeke.
